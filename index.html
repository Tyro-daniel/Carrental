Yes — you can cancel or modify your booking free of charge up to 48 hours before your scheduled pickup. Cancellations within 48 hours may incur a small fee depending on the vehicle category.

All vehicles are provided with a full tank of fuel. We ask that you return the car with a full tank. If the vehicle is returned with less fuel, a refuelling fee will be charged based on the amount needed.

The minimum rental age is 21 years old. Drivers between 21 and 24 may be subject to a young driver surcharge, which varies by vehicle class. Drivers 25 and above are not affected by any age surcharge.

We offer three coverage tiers — Basic, Standard, and Premium. Basic includes third-party liability and theft protection. Standard adds collision damage waiver and zero excess. Premium covers everything including windscreen, tyres, and international use.
